# IoT Applied to Intelligent Electrical Systems

The advancement of digital technologies has promoted significant transformations in modern electrical systems, especially in the areas of monitoring, automation and remote supervision.

In this context, the Internet of Things (IoT) enables the integration between sensors, embedded systems and communication technologies, allowing real-time acquisition and transmission of electrical data.

The use of IoT in electrical systems contributes to intelligent monitoring of parameters such as voltage, current and power, enabling fault detection, operational supervision and predictive maintenance applications.

In addition, smart monitoring systems can improve operational reliability, energy efficiency and fault response time in electrical installations and industrial environments.

Therefore, IoT represents an important technological solution for the modernization of electrical systems and development of intelligent energy infrastructures.
