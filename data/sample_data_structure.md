# Sample Data Structure

## Objective

Define the structure for electrical data acquisition and storage.

---

## Parameters to Monitor

- Voltage
- Current
- Power
- Frequency
- Temperature

---

## Data Collection

The ESP32 will acquire electrical signals through sensors and transmit data for storage and analysis.

---

## Storage Format

CSV files will be used for initial tests.

Example:

| Timestamp | Voltage | Current | Power |
|-----------|----------|----------|-------|
| 10:00:01 | 220V | 5A | 1100W |

---

## Future Applications

- Fault detection
- Data analysis
- Predictive maintenance
- Intelligent monitoring
